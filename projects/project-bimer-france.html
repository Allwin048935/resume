BIMer Services France

About BIMer Services:

BIMer Services is a French company based in Montpellier, specializing in Building Information Modeling for Operation and Maintenance (BIM GEM). They offer innovative digital solutions to enhance the management of real estate assets. Their services include the creation of digital twins, BIM charters, and maintenance logs, all adhering to the ISO 16739 standard to ensure data ownership and software independence. BIMer Services supports property managers and public housing organizations across France in digitizing and optimizing their building portfolios.

Project Details

  • Location: Montpellier, France
  • Duration: 2022 - 2023
  • LOD: 300
  • Role: Revit Architecture BIM Modeler
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
  • Tools Used: Revit, IFC Viewer, Dynamo (basic usage), AutoCAD, MS Excel, MS Word
  • Reference: Project Reference

Key Contributions

  • Developed as-built Revit models for residential and commercial buildings, including architectural and landscape modeler.
  • Used Dynamo for automating sheet creation, schedule creating, place the schedules with respective sheets, quantity takeoffs, improving efficiency and reduce the manual interuptions.
  • Check the data match with standards to ensure model accuracy.
  • Ensured IFC-compliant models for seamless data exchange with French stakeholders.

Challenges and Solutions

Adapting to French building standards required extensive coordination. I conducted thorough reviews with local teams and used AutoCAD for 2D drafting to align with client requirements, ensuring model compliance.

Outcomes

The accurate as-built project supported facility management and renovation planning, delivering value to clients and meeting project deadlines.
