King Salman Park Utilities

Overview of King Salman Park:

King Salman Park is a transformative urban development project located in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Spanning over 16 square kilometers, it is set to become the world's largest urban park. The park aims to enhance the quality of life for residents and visitors by offering extensive green spaces, cultural venues, sports facilities, and entertainment options. Key features include the Royal Arts Complex, the Visitor Pavilion, and a 7.2-kilometer pedestrian loop. As a flagship initiative under Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, King Salman Park embodies the Kingdom's commitment to sustainability, cultural enrichment, and urban revitalization.

Project Details

  • Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
  • Duration: 2023 - 2024
  • Role: Senior BIM Modeler
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
  • Tools Used: Civil 3D, Navisworks, AutoCAD
  • Reference: Project Reference

Key Contributions

  • Modeled extensive utility networks, including water supply, sewerage, and electrical systems, using Civil 3D.
  • Created labels, profile bands for profile views, and pipe catalogs.
  • Conducted clash detection with Navisworks to eliminate conflicts across disciplines and internal clashes.
  • Used Data Shortcuts to resolve clashes and maintain minimum clearance between other services.

Challenges and Solutions

The project’s scale required managing large datasets. I optimized model performance by implementing data shortcuts in Civil 3D, ensuring timely delivery of high-quality models.

Outcomes

The BIM models enabled efficient utility planning, reducing on-site issues and supporting the park’s development as a sustainable public space.
