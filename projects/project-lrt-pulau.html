The Mutiara Line LRT

Overview of LRT:

The Mutiara Line LRT is a Light Rail Transit project under development in Penang, Malaysia, connecting George Town's city center with its southern suburbs and a link to Seberang Perai. This 29.5 km line aims to improve public transport and ease road congestion by providing faster, more efficient routes to major destinations.

Project Details

  • Location: Penang, Malaysia
  • Duration: Sep 2024 - Ongoing
  • LOD: 300
  • Role: BIM Modeler
  • Worked Firm: HSS BIM Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
  • Tools Used: Revit, Civil3D, Dynamo (basic usage), AutoCAD
  • Reference: Project Reference

Key Contributions

  • Modeled existing and proposed dry and wet utilities over an 18.5 km stretch, covering 11 metro stations.
  • Used Dynamo in Revit to automate the placement of drains in station areas.
  • Ensured data compliance with the standards outlined in the BEP (BIM Execution Plan).
  • Conducted frequent clash detections using Navisworks with other disciplines to ensure a clash-free model.

Challenges and Solutions

Coordinating utility modeling along an extensive corridor with multiple stakeholders was a major challenge. While metro bridges, stations, and MEP systems were handled by other teams, I focused on accurately modeling dry and wet utilities across road and station areas. Frequent coordination meetings, strict adherence to BEP standards, and consistent clash detection reviews using Navisworks helped ensure seamless integration with other disciplines and a clash-free model.

Outcomes

Successfully delivered accurate and coordinated utility models across the 18.5 km alignment, supporting the overall development of the LRT corridor. My contributions enabled smoother integration with architectural, structural, and MEP models by proactively resolving clashes and aligning with BEP standards. Automation using Dynamo improved efficiency in repetitive tasks such as drain placement, reducing manual efforts and minimizing errors. The collaborative approach with other stakeholders ensured steady project progress and model reliability for future construction phases.
